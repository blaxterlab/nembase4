CladeIV nematodes
Clade IV includes both freeliving and parasitic nematodes. The are vertebrate parasites, while the Tylenchida include many major plant parasites. The clade also includes pathogens of insects, and the freeliving Cephalobina and Panagrolaimida.

Strongyloides ratti
 S. ratti is a rodent parasite used as a model of the human parasite S. stercoralis. ESTs are being sequenced in Washington university St Louis.

 S. stercoralis is a human parasite infecting over 0.5 billion people. ESTs are being sequenced in Washington university St Louis.
