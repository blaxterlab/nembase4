BLAST v Uniprot
Top hit from a BLAST search with the consensus sequence against
the Uinprot database.  
Quoted score is the BLAST e-value. 
http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/BLAST/
http://www.ebi.uniprot.org/

GOtcha (slim)
The fourth level Gene Ontology (GO) term, asigned by GOtcha, 
with the highest confidence.
Quoted score is the GOtcha confidence where 100 is highly 
confident and 1 is not confident.

KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes
Top hit from a BLAST search with the predicted protein sequence
against the KEGG pathway database.
Quoted score is the BLAST e-value. 
http://www.genome.jp/kegg/

InterProScan
Search of multiple databases with the predicted protein 
sequence using either HMMER or BLAST search method to find 
protein domains and functional sites.
Quoted score is the search method e-value.
http://www.ebi.ac.uk/InterProScan/
